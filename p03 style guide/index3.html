IAT222 Interactive Arts:

In this class, we as a team are asked to produce a work of interactive arts. This work will be displayed at SIAT, and it has to reflect artist practice in at least one area of studied in class. First step: as a group, we signed a team contract. We made rules within the team, for example, what will we do if one team member missed a meeting, or missed a presentation.

Second step:

The team had to determine the overall topic for the artwork. Additionally, we had to choose a scope which interested all team members while reflecting team inspirations. This helped decide our final project goal.

Third step:

We introduced our artwork proposal. First the team decided on a single topic after discussing what we wanted to present within the final artwork. Other areas were considered, such as how to make the piece work. For instance, utilizing sketches for showing how the final piece should appear, ways it could interact with audience members, and what type of feeling it should create.

Forth/Fifth step:

The artwork prototype. The team began working and encounter several problems. First, the professor stated our prototype was too simple. Therefore, we had to discussed and rethink of alternative methods for participants to interact with the final artwork.

Final project in the Gallery. Our final artwork was showcased at SFU. To ensure participants understood, we included a description with the final piece. We lacked enough gold paper during the final stage, therefore, we decided to apply it only on two sides of the box’s interior. Instead of four sides.

PUB 375:

This is a project from Pub 375, the goal of this project is to build up a real magazine through the whole semester, we were working in group of five, two of us from IAT program are responsible for design the magazine.

Step 1:

The first step of design the real magazine is to choose a color palate that is suitable for our magazine’s theme. As a group, we decided that the main idea of our magazine is about travel, and the mood that tourist have during their journeys.

Step 2:

As the design group of this project, the second step is to design a tittle for the magazine. Since our magazine is about travel, the name we discussed as a group is “the resident nomad”. We have created several versions of the tittle of magazine, then in our group, we vote for which one is better for the magazine.

Step 3:

he third step is to design the cover page of our magazine, since the cover page is the first thing that people see, so we have to establish the mood, and the concept for our magazine, We designed three cover pages for the magazine, and then as a group, decided on which one we should use for our cover page.

Step 4:

As the designer of the group, we have to design for the print publication and the web layout for our magazine, and also the poster for our launch party.
