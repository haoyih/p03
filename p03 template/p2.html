Project 2: Process Analysis

PUB 375

This is a project from Pub 375, the goal of this project is to build up a real magazine through the whole semester, we were working in group of five, two of us from IAT program are responsible for design the magazine.

01

Step 1

The first step of design the real magazine is to choose a color palate that is suitable for our magazine’s theme. As a group, we decided that the main idea of our magazine is about travel, and the mood that tourist have during their journeys.

02

Step 2

As the design group of this project, the second step is to design a tittle for the magazine. Since our magazine is about travel, the name we discussed as a group is “the resident nomad”. We have created several versions of the tittle of magazine, then in our group, we vote for which one is better for the magazine.

03

Step 3

The third step is to design the cover page of our magazine, since the cover page is the first thing that people see, so we have to establish the mood, and the concept for our magazine, We designed three cover pages for the magazine, and then as a group, decided on which one we should use for our cover page.

Step 4

As the designer of the group,

we have to design for the print publication and the web layout for our magazine,

and also the poster for our launch party.

Final Step

We have our physical magazine printed, and present it in the class with all the real content inside.

In this group, we all come from different background with different level. There are two designers in our group, the other designer was busy this semester, the work she had done is not satisfied our group’s expectation. I talk to her, and re-divided the work, and help her with the work that she is not capable of doing. From working with other student in my group, I learned that we have to help each other, we cannot just finish our parts and left, and it is important to communicate with each other, to see what they are thinking about the work you have done, if there is a problem, then as a team we can solve it together, we do not have to face the problem alone.
